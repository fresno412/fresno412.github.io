Our Story

As a family owned and operated ranch we take pride in every aspect of raising our grass fed cows. We breed our cattle to offer the highest quality beef and grow our own special grasses and hay to ensure the best feed. We are small enough to hand count our cattle and offer you the opportunity to pick your cow and cut. We’re happy to offer bundled cuts to ensure delicious options — from premium ground beef to top sirloin at reasonable ‘direct from the farm’ prices.

“Small enough to know the herd by heart—and focused enough to get every detail right.”

Have questions? Call or text us and we’ll walk you through availability, timing, and pickup.
